Hello! Welcome to an average class with Silaghi.

In this game, you will need to get through a class with Silaghi as your
professor. This means solving a quiz while paying attention to attendance,
going through a lab while listening to a lecture, and avoiding what obstacles
may come your way.

The screen is split in two, the left is whatever you're solving (quiz/lab) and
the right is Silaghi's dialogue. To switch between one screen and the other,
press TAB. To navigate, use the arrow keys.

Score will be based on how well you solve the quiz and lab, your attendance
record, and how late you handed in your work.

You will notice there is a timer at the bottom of the screen. This is how much
time you have left to solve the quiz. It will switch to the lab timer when you
start it. You'll notice its currently running. That's because you're late to
class...

If you are still reading this, you're already doing poorly. Press Enter to
start the quiz you're not going to solve in time. Good luck!
