Welcome to the game's syllabus.

If you opened this before pressing start: well done.
If not, I understand why you're here.
Just like a Silaghi class, you should go into the game prepared.

The game consists of multiple tasks you must complete.
Quiz: You must complete a quiz. Select question with arrows, type
    with keyboard and press enter to complete. You lose points for
	wrong answers and for turning it in late.
Lab: Very similar to quiz. Slightly harder but you have more time.
Fetching/Returning: To get a quiz/lab or to return it, you must dodge
    your fellow classmates in a dino-style game. Press space to jump.
	Getting hit results in time loss.
Attendance: As the class goes on the professor will take attendance.
    when your name appears, quickly press space-bar or you'll lose
	points.
